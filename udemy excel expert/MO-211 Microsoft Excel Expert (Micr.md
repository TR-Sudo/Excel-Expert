Prepare workbooks for collaboration

&nbsp;	**Restrict editing**

&nbsp;		under the top bar you can press "protect sheet" and a wide verity of options are available to limit editing, like individual cells or whole rows or columns



&nbsp;		you can view protected into in the info section of the excel file



&nbsp;		you can stop certain cells from being able to be locked by going into the font settings and under protection uncheck "locked"



&nbsp;	under "allow edit ranges" you can make selected cells editable by using a different assigned password or even allow users who logged into certain accounts to edit without password



&nbsp;	**Protect workbook structure**

		under protect workbook from the top menu you can stop users from being able to change, reorder, delete or insert new sheets into the excel file
